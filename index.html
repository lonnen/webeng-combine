2014 Webeng Combine

A series of programming puzzles for the attendees of the WebEng 2014 work.

Grab the challenge here.

Download and unpack it somewhere. Inside you'll find a test harness, some puzzles, and a folder called "reference". The puzzles are detailed here on this website. Write your solutions to the problem, make sure they have the same name as the puzzle, and drop them in /reference. Then execute the testrunner like so:

runtests /puzzles /reference

Use the shebang line to write your answer in something other than bash or binary.
